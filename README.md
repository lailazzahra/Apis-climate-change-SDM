**Projection of *Apis dorsata* Distribution in Southeast Asia Under Moderate (SSP245) and High (SSP585) Emmisions by 2050**



**Overview**

This project investigates how climate change may affect the distribution of *Apis dorsata* across Southeast Asia using Species Distribution Modeling (SDM).



**Data Sources**

1. GBIF occurrence records
2. WorldClim 2.1 bioclimatic variables
3. CMIP6 future climate projections



**Methods**

1. Random Forest classification
2. Presence-background modeling approach
3. Climate predictors: BIO1 (Annual Mean Temperature), BIO4 (Temperature Seasonality), BIO12 (Annual Precipitation), BIO15 (Precipitation Seasonality)



**Results**

|**Scenario**|**Suitable Cells**|**Change (%)**|
|-|-|-|
|Current|3131|0|
|SSP245|1621|-48.2|
|SSP585|1801|-42.5|



Most important predictor: BIO4 (Temperature Seasonality)
